# 📰 AutoNews

AutoNews is a Python-based news processing system that fetches ~1500 news articles, filters and analyzes them, and delivers curated content focused on semiconductor, India trade, and policy updates for company weekly newsletters.

## 🚀 Features

* Fetches large-scale news data (~1500 articles)
* Intelligent filtering and categorization
* Focus on:

  * Semiconductor news
  * India trade news
  * India policy updates
* Automatic summarization (no need to read full articles)
* Outputs curated content for newsletters


## 📌 Use Case

Designed for generating relevant and concise news content for internal company weekly newsletters.
